Questions

Before you open a case

Is a verdict here proof that something is AI-generated?

No — and we say so on purpose. This is a probabilistic second opinion, not forensic proof. Text verdicts are a model's read of the writing; image verdicts combine hard metadata (which can be close to conclusive when present) with a vision model's read (which, like text, is probabilistic).

What happens to my 3 free examinations?

They're tied to your account, not a time window — use them whenever. Once they're used, the examiner stays visible but locked until you upgrade.

Do you store the text or images I submit?

No. Content is examined and discarded — we keep the verdict record (for your History), not the original submission.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Pro is a monthly plan with no lock-in — cancel from Settings and you won't be charged again.
